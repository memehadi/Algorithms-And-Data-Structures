Assuming I had 11 slots on my table,I used the formula 
slot= ((k%11)+i*(k%9))%11
This function is suitable as given that 11 is a prime number and
I used a slightly less number(9) on my secondary function,
I will have a constant interval probing and an evenly distributed Hash table.
